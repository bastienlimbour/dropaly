# Instructions for AI Agents

## Workflow Conventions

### Documentation

Read `docs/agents/documentation.md` before exploring the codebase or working on a task.

### Issue Tracker

Read `docs/agents/issue-tracker.md` if:

- Your task involves working with issues or the issue tracker.
- Your task involves creating, reading, or listing issues.
- Your task involves commenting or applying/removing labels on an issue.
- The user or a skill explicitly asks to "create an issue", "read the issue", "publish on the issue tracker", "fetch the relevant ticket", or any other issue-related task.

### Local Artifacts

Read `docs/agents/local-artifacts.md` if:

- Your task involves working with local markdown artifacts, such as `brainstorming.md`, `brief.md`, or `validation.md`.
- Your task involves creating, reading, or updating local markdown artifacts.
- The user or a skill explicitly mentions "local artifact", "local markdown artifact", "local initiative artifact", or any other local artifact-related term.
